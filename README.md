# ☕ Coffee Personality Quiz

A personality quiz that reveals your perfect coffee match—built with AI.

## What is this?

Answer 5 simple lifestyle questions and discover which coffee personality matches how you live. It's that simple.

**Built with:** Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript

## How it works

1. **Take the quiz** — 5 questions about your morning routine, vacation style, downtime, work style, and values
2. **We tally your answers** — Each answer maps to a personality type
3. **See your match** — Get your top coffee personality and a breakdown of all 4 types

## The coffee types

- **Bold Adventurer** → Double Espresso — You live for intensity
- **Zen Minimalist** → Black Coffee, Single Origin — Simple. Clean. Perfect.
- **Social Butterfly** → Cappuccino — Coffee is better with company
- **Health Nut** → Oat Milk Americano — Wellness in every sip
